Cafca Synthetic Face Dataset

A large-scale, diverse multiview face dataset for avatar reconstruction and animation, face parsing, synthetic-to-real transfer learning, animation, relighting, camera estimation, and more.

Please use the links in the navigation bar to quickly jump to sections.

Dataset Facts

  • 1,755 Mio. images
  • 1,500 diverse identities x 13 expressions x 3 environments x 30 views
  • Resolution 512x512
  • Annotations for 14 semantic regions
  • Perspective OpenCV cameras
  • Pseudo-GT keypoints and FLAME 2023 annotations & meshes (fitted with VHAP)
  • CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 License

Related Datasets

The following synthetic datasets could also be useful:

  • DAViD (ICCV 2025): a synthetic dataset with 100K images for each of these body regions: face, upper-body and full-body.
  • BEDLAM (CVPR 2023): a synthetic dataset with 10K image full-body sequences.
  • IMAvatar (CVPR 2022 oral): a synthetic face dataset with 10 identities.
